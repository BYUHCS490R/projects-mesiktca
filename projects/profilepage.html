Hi! My name is Missy. I really enjoy the outdoors. You won't catch me hiking to the peak of the mountain. But you would find me looking up at the nightsky absolutely mesmerized. ☽ Something about the infinite space above me makes me feel free. I love the sunrise and sunsets. If I'm not having a good day being in nature will cure me. Here are some things I hope for in regards to my love for nature.

  1. Own a little sustainable farm
  2. Own a nice big telescope
  3. See the northern lights

I also really love the Holiday season. I love Easter and the 4th, but the fall Holiday season takes the cake. I'm a sucker for all things pumpkin spice related. The neighborhood I grew up in threw block parties. One house sold the most amazing apple cider for 75¢. Dr. Spendlove claimed to have a super secret sauce he used- just to tease the kids. I love reading the Harry Potter series and The Lord of the Rings. I enjoy going to haunted houses and corn mazes. I love the Christmas spirit. It warms my heart to see people donating and getting involved in community service during the Holiday season.
