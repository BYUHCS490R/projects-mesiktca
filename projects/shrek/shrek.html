Shrek is a filmed released in the early 2000s about an ogre and a princess and their journey together. In the kingdom, magical creatures are begin persecuted and taken to other places. Shrek and Donkey set out to fix this problem leading them to rescueing Princess Fiona.

Their journey doesn't end there. Princess Fiona is not happy with the man she is set to marry and nothing changes for magical creatures. Shrek, Fiona, and Donkey come together to overcome this challenges.
